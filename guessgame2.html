About Me

My name is Alina To. I'm a freelance violinist, ex-university English faculty, and aspiring web developer. I've been living on the same block in the Capitol Hill neighborhood in 3 different buildings (4 different units) for 10 years. I love my neightbors and my neighborhood, although I don't love all the recent developments. When I'm not complaining on the sidewalk with my neighbors about the extreme rent increase like we're New Yorkers, I'm performing around town in different groups at different venues.

Goals

I hope to learn and practice as much as I can from this course, take a few more courses, and land a job as a front-end web developer (while maintaining my music life). I've been studying by myself and playing around, and hope to completely revamp my personal music website.
